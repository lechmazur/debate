[C1.3] Conflating data analytics with price-fixing would chill legitimate market research. [C1.4] The competitive effects differ fundamentally from collusion. Cartels artificially restrict supply to sustain supracompetitive prices. [C1.5] PRO invites us to treat informed pricing as if it were a smoke-filled room. But lacking mutual commitment to adhere to the algorithm’s output, these arrangements remain the unilateral use of market information—a practice essential to competitive markets, not destructive of them.
