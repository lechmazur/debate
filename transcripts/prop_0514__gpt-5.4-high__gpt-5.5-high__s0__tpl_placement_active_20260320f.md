[P1.2] The public should not have to ask a single vendor for permission to move them.
[P1.3] Open standards turn procurement from a one-shot sale into a contest vendors must keep winning. If records, documents, APIs, and export formats are portable, a district can replace the learning platform without losing all coursework history, and a hospital can change appointment or analytics tools without rebuilding the entire estate. That disciplines pricing, prevents forced bundle purchases, and lets smaller specialized suppliers compete. [P1.5] In healthcare especially, trapped data and brittle migrations are not just admin headaches; they can disrupt care. In schools, they can strand records, attendance, special-needs documentation, and parent communication.
