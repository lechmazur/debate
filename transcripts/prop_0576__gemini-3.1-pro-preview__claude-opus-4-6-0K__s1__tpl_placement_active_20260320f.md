[P1.2] First, taxpayer dollars should not fund partisan campaigns. Every dollar a religious organization does not pay in taxes is a dollar that must be made up by the broader public. The state has no business financing political machinery under the guise of religious liberty.
[P1.3] Second, allowing religious groups to retain tax exemptions while operating politically creates a dangerous loophole in campaign finance. [P1.4] Wealthy donors could funnel tax-deductible dark money through religious institutions to influence elections, completely bypassing the rules that govern every other political actor in our democracy.
