[P1.2] Life-extension programs for existing reactors are the fastest, cheapest path to clean power we have. [P1.3] New nuclear builds, while capital-intensive, provide unmatched carbon displacement per installed megawatt. [P1.4] The hidden cost of renewables is the system cost: batteries, transmission, and backup generation needed for reliability. [P1.5] Public dollars should chase results, not promises. Every dollar spent extending a nuclear plant’s life guarantees a specific, immediate flow of zero-emission electricity.
