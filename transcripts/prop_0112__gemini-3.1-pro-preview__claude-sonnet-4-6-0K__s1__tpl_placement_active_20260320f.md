[C1.7] The central issue this debate must resolve is whether the defects of employer-based systems are so irreparable that they justify transferring all financing authority to a single public entity — one with no competitive pressure to improve, no exit option for dissatisfied patients, and no pricing feedback to allocate scarce resources.
