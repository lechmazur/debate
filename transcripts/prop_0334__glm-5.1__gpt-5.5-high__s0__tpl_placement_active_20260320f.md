A doctored video clip becomes harmful not because it exists but because viewers lack the footage showing what happened before and after. Context systems insert that corrective information where people are already looking rather than driving them elsewhere.

[P1.5] Takedowns have a further structural defect: they're binary. A piece of content is either gone or untouched.
