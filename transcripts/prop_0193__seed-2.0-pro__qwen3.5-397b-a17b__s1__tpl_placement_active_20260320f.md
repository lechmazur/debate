[P1.3] For browsers and AI assistants, default lock-in lets OS providers hoard user search and interaction data to further entrench their ad and AI monopolies, while privacy-focused alternatives, accessibility-optimized tools, and startup-built specialized services never gain the user base needed to iterate and compete.
