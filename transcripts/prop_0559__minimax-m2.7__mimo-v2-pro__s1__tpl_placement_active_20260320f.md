[C1.6] Once data becomes portable, controlling downstream misuse becomes nearly impossible—something we have learned painfully through major breaches and scandals involving third-party data exploitation.
[C1.7] Consider the innovation incentive problem. Platforms invest heavily in network-building because those networks constitute their competitive advantage.
