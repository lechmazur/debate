[C1.2] The "materially higher" threshold is fundamentally unworkable. There is no widely agreed standard for what counts as a material price difference: 10%? 20%? $5? $10? [C1.4] Contrary to claims that this helps independent restaurants, this mandate would actively harm them. Apps already surface pickup options for users who explicitly select that mode; mandating forced promotion of external alternatives during a delivery checkout is a compelled subsidy to rival ordering channels, not consumer protection.
