[P1.4] GDP is silent on these disasters; a well-being framework forces them into the spotlight. Choosing the headline target determines what gets measured, and what gets measured dictates what gets managed. We must manage for what truly matters—the health and happiness of our people—not for a ledger that mistakes activity for achievement.
