[C1.5] Trusting this theoretical instrument to justify a blanket default is building policy on a product that does not yet reliably exist.

The proposition eliminates that judgment wholesale.
[C1.7] PRO frames opposition to this motion as "blanket prejudice." But precision runs both ways. A rule that ignores building type, tenant mix, and coverage adequacy is not precision—it is a different kind of bluntness masquerading as sophistication.
