[C1.4] Second, the enforcement mechanism the proposition requires would produce systematic viewpoint discrimination. [C1.5] Third, the underlying tax logic is weaker than PRO assumes. [C1.6] The real question is not whether we like political churches. The question is whether giving regulators this power creates dangers that outweigh any benefit. It does.
