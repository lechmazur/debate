[P1.2] When evidence on academic gains is mixed, that uncertainty cuts both ways: it means we cannot justify trapping students in geographically assigned schools on the promise that those schools are objectively superior. Dollars are appropriated to educate children, not to subsidize buildings, legacy staffing, or administrative bloat.
